# ☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing coffee sales performance across different cities, products, and time periods.

The dashboard helps identify sales trends, product performance, discount impact, and geographic distribution of sales.

## Analysis Included

### Sales by City

* Visualizes coffee sales across different cities using a map.
* Helps identify high-performing locations.

### Sales by Product

* Compares sales distribution among:

  * Colombian
  * Costa Rica
  * Ethiopian
  * Guatemala
  * Brazilian

### Monthly Sales Trends

* Tracks final sales across months and quarters.
* Highlights seasonal fluctuations.

### Discount Analysis

* Compares quantities sold with and without discounts.
* Evaluates the impact of discount campaigns.

### Sales by Day and Product

* Analyzes product performance throughout the week.
* Identifies peak sales days.

## Key Insights

* Colombian and Costa Rica coffee products generated the highest sales.
* Sales peaked during mid-year periods.
* Discount campaigns contributed significantly to total sales volume.
* Sales performance varied across cities and weekdays.
